Ceylon Cinnamon — Signature Spice

Ceylon (Cinnamomum verum) is distinct from cassia: it delivers a delicate aroma and lower coumarin levels — making it the premium choice for health-conscious buyers.

Why Choose Ceylon?

  • Authentic botanical variety (Cinnamomum verum) with sweet-spicy notes.
  • Lowest coumarin content; preferred for food & nutraceutical use.
  • Smallholder cultivation with traditional hand-peeling processing.
  • Traceability available with batch-level origin data.

Applications

  • Retail spice jars and premium bakery/confectionery.
  • Essential oils, natural extracts and wellness products.

Packaging & Quality

Hand-graded quills and moisture-controlled packing. We support regulatory documentation for EU and Middle East exports.
