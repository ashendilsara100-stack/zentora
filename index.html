Ceylon Cinnamon — Signature Spice

Distinct from Cassia, True Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum) offers a delicate aroma and safety for health-conscious buyers.

Why Choose Ceylon Cinnamon?

  • Authentic botanical variety with sweet-spicy notes.
  • Lowest coumarin content; safe for daily consumption.
  • Traditional hand-peeling preserves premium quality.
  • Traceability available for premium global buyers.

Primary Applications

  • Retail spice jars & high-end bakery.
  • Natural extracts and essential oil production.
  • Nutraceuticals and health supplements.

Export Quality

Moisture-controlled packing and private-label options available for EU and Middle East exports.
